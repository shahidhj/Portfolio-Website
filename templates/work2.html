001:Early Detection

Early detection Breast Cancer risks aims at identifying and segmenting breast cancer tumours there by dramatically increasing their chance of survival

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Analysis, Design and Modelling the entire Data pre processing pipeline
  • Setting up the AWS infrastructure
  • Medical Imaging analysis and preparation
  • Research different architectures and build state of the art models
  • Experimentation using different architectures, transfer learning and using 3D Convolutions
  • Tweaking and Fine tuning the models to improve the accuracy and reduced the false negatives
  • The entire Data pre processing, modelling and training of the neural nets was done using pytorch

Being part of the Early detection team at BC Cancer Research was an honour. Great experience in understanding and applying Deep learning to medical imaging with a ton of learning.
