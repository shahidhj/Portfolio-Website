002:Estate Handling

Estate Handling(Bobehandling) is a product of Danske Bank which aims at handling the assets, estates of the deceased customers.

Roles and Responsibilities for this project included

  • Lead Developer of the first team at Danske Bank to carry out a proof of concept with IBM on using the new Case Management Product at Danske Bank
  • GEMBA sessions to understand the AS-IS process and requirement gathering
  • Analysis, Design of wire frames and Integration services
  • Actively involved in Sprint Planning
  • Front end development using IBM Case Manager, development and testing of Rest Services

Being part of the first team at Danske Bank to test an entirely new product was a wonderful experience with a ton of learnings along the way
